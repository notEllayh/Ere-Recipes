Fluffy Pancake Recipe

Fluffy pancakes are light, airy, and soft with a slightly golden, crispy edge. Made with a simple batter of flour, milk, eggs, and a leavening agent like baking powder, they puff up beautifully when cooked. These pancakes are the perfect base for toppings like syrup, butter, fresh fruit, or whipped cream, making them a delicious and comforting breakfast treat.
